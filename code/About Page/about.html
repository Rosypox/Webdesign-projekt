About Me

I'm a 3D artist and computer science engineer specialising in product rendering,
animation and hard-surface modelling.
I strive to give each and every one of my clients an individual approach by getting to know their brand and needs. I'm here to help your online marketing stand out from the competition with the power of 3D and CGI!

story

What started as a college project in a
tiny bedroom, grew to be a brand I
wanted to be identified with. The
company grew from a passion for
3D and artistic innovation which I
stayed true all the way through my
computer science studies up to this
day. I've always loved working with
people so creating something for
others seemed like the right way
forward. At Cubify we treat every day
as a new challenge to learn and
develop as artists which we hope to
continue doing for many years to
come.
